About Christ University

History

Since 1969, Christ (Deemed to be University) originated from the educational vision of St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara in 1969 as Christ College. Granted autonomy by the UGC in 2004, it was recognized for excellence in 2006. In 2008, it became a "deemed to be university," and in 2018, the term "university" was dropped as per Supreme Court direction. It was one of the first institutions in India accredited by NAAC in 1998, consistently receiving Grade 'A+' in 2004, 2016, and 2022.

Christ is under the management of the priests of the Catholic religious order, Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), part of Syro Malabar Major Archiepiscopal Church. In 2016, the university was accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council with A+ Grade.
